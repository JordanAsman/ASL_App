Credits

Jordan Asman created this app for her Senior AP Research capstone project.
She wanted to accommodate the Deaf community in museum attractions, so she
thought the best way to do this would be to create her own app. She used her
background in American Sign Language along with her math and science skills
to create this app. She wanted to make sure that the app had proper ASL word
order, so she translated and recorded the videos herself. She hopes that this
app is helpful and that you enjoy using it.
